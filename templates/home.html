Who we are?

We intend to provide a free-of-cost online skin consultation system with skin disease prediction.

The user can get highly specialized advice regarding his/her issue through our chatbot. If the user wants, the user can also upload an image of the part of the body which is affected by the disease and then our system will predict the disease and will prescribe the list of medicines and ointments needed to tackle the skin issue. Through our website, users can get highly specialized advice about their skin problems through our chatbot anytime and get medicines and ointments prescribed to tackle their problems within a matter of seconds without them stepping outside of their homes

Is it possible to solve skin or hair issues in ?

Yes. Most skin diseases can be treated as picture is enough to diagnose for majority skin diseases. However, there are certain cases that must be examined in clinic. If we feel that your case can not be solved online, we will let you know that & will ask you to visit a good dermatologist nearby.

What type of skin problems can be treated online ?

Most skin and hair problems including acne, pigmentation, tanning, vitiligo, rosacea, fungal infection, melasma, acanthosis nigricans, hair fall, allergy & urticaria etc can be treated online.

How long will it take?

We usually reply within a few hours, maximum 24 hours. As it happens within a chat, there is no wastage of time. You answer when you are free and we answer when we are free.

How this process will work?

For our chat service, You start by sending us a message. Then in most cases, we ask you to upload some photo.
Then we may send you some questions to answer. We usually start with one or two cream/ shampoo/ lotion etc that you may buy from any medical shops. We will share a full prescription if required.
Phone/video call is usually not required (very rarely may be required if felt so by the doctor).

Are the doctors qualified here?

All our doctors are MD in dermatology. Unlike many other portals, we do not include any alternate medicine doctors.

How do I pay for my online visit ?

You can start with 1 Rs only.
Then if full prescription is required, it will cost 149 INR*.
If your case does not require a prescription or it can not be solved online, no fee will be asked.
*5 USD or equivalent for international patients.

How much is your fee ?

For Indian patients, 149 INR / prescription. For international patients, it is 5 USD or equivalent at present.

Is my photo etc secured in WhatsApp ?

Yes.
WhatsApp is end to end encrypted. Moreover, if it’s a sensitive photo (like a photo of private parts), you can simply delete it from your end and it will be deleted from the chat.
However, for most normal consults, we recommend that you keep the photo so that it helps during the next visit. No photo is needed to use our intelligent tools service!

What if I have doubts later?

Once you send a message in WhatsApp, later you can alsways ask any questions in the WhatsApp thread.
